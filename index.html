About Me

I began my academic journey in Game Engineering at Kempten University of Applied Sciences in the winter semester of 2018/19, successfully completing my Bachelor's degree in March 2023. My first significant experience with the Unity Engine came during the summer semester of 2020.

During the winter semester of 2020/21, I completed my practical semester as a Unity Developer at TriCAT. This full-time position over six months provided hands-on experience and significantly enhanced my development skills.

Building on this foundation, I pursued a Master's degree in Game Engineering and Visual Computing at Kempten University of Applied Sciences starting in the winter semester of 2022/23. I recently completed this program in March 2025, earning my Master of Science degree.

While completing my Master's studies, I worked as a Working Student at Siemens from April 2023 to January 2025. This role allowed me to further develop my Unity skills while also gaining experience in web development, particularly WebXR development.
